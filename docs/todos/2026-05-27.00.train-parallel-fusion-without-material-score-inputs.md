## Context

The clean-data parallel fusion diagnostic suggests the MaterialFeatureValueHead may contribute to overconfident failures in sharp tactical/endgame cases. The next controlled architecture test should remove explicit material-score-derived inputs entirely, not merely weaken them.

## Why It Is Needed

Validation-prefix diagnostics showed the parallel model was especially worse in confident-label and moderate material-imbalance buckets. Source tracing later showed that material/label sign conflict is present but not dominant, so removing material inputs is now an isolation experiment rather than a proven fix.

## Completion Criteria

## Detailed Plan
