## Why It Is Needed

If empirical labels are poorly calibrated against raw material difference, fixed material-prior scaling may inject systematic residual burden and optimization spikes. The analysis should quantify whether labels follow material score as expected, where they diverge, and whether divergence explains the current experiment behavior.
